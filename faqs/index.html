Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery take to charge?

A fully discharged battery will charge to 80% capacity in 40 minutes. It will fully charge in 50 minutes. A gauge on the charger illustrates charge status.

How long will the battery hold a charge?

At the “high” (rabbit) setting, the battery has 30 minutes of cutting time. At the “low” (turtle) setting, the battery has 50 minutes of cutting time. Be sure to always use ECHO SilenTwist trimmer line to get maximum cutting time.

How do I know how much charge is left?

A series of indicator lights is on the battery. Simply press the button and remaining charge is indicated. Four illuminated dots is a full charge.

What if I leave the switch on?

Always turn the unit off when finished to avoid accidental operation.

What is the battery’s life span?

The battery’s life is dependent on the user’s individual circumstances. It is covered by the 3-year consumer warranty.
